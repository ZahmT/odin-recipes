Lemon Pepper Chicken

Dairy Free Lemon Pepper Chicken

Tender chicken breasts in a creamy, and craveable lemon pepper sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breasts
  • salt and pepper to season
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil, divided
  • 1 large shallot, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground pepper
  • 1 cup chicken bone broth (or chicken broth) + 2 tbsp
  • 13 oz coconut milk
  • 1 lemon, juice and zest
  • 1 tbsp arrowroot starch
  • for serving: prepared rice, arugula, and chopped fresh parsley
  1. Season your chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper
  2. Heat 2 tbsp of avocado oil in a large skillet over medium high heat
  3. Add your chicken to the skillet and sear on both sides for a 4-5 minutes until nice and golden
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet, lower the heat to medium, and add another sbsp of avocado oil
  5. Add the shallot and saute for 2-3 minutes until softened
  6. Add the garlic and pepper and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant
  7. Add your chicken bone broth and deglaze the pan, scraping up any bits that have stuck to the bottom
  8. Add the coconut milk, lemon juice, and lemon zest and stir to combine
  9. Return the chicken to the skillet, bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook for 10-12 minutes
  10. Uncover and push your chicken breasts to the side
  11. Make a slurry with your arrowroot starch by combining the starch with 2 tbsp of chicken bone broth, stirring so there are no lumps
  12. Whisk the slurry into the sauce, stir everything together, basting the chicken witht he sauce
  13. Remove from heat and serve with lemon slice, more fresh cracked pepper, and chopped fresh parsley
  14. Enjoy!
