Protean Peanut Butter Cup Creami

Yummy Peanut Butter Cup Ice Cream

Dairy free protein peanutbutter cup creami ice cream

Ingredients

  • 1 can lite coconut milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/4 cup powdered peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp. maple syrup
  • 1/8 tsp. kosher sea salt
  • 1/2 cup dair-free chocolate peanut butter cups, halved
  • Ninja Creami

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients except for the peanut butter cups to a blender and blend
  2. Pour the mixture into the Ninja Creami contianer
  3. Store in the freezer overnight
  4. Remove the frozen Creami container from the freezer and place it in the Creami machine and hit the "ice cream" setting
  5. Remove the Creami container from the machine and add 1 tbsp of nut milk
  6. Place back in the machine with the lid on and select "respin"
  7. Perform this respin process 2x until the mixture is creamy and smooth
  8. Stir a 1/3 cup of the peanut butter cups into the ice cream
  9. Place the Creami back in the dock and select "mix-ins"
  10. Once it has spun a final time, remove and serve with more peanut butter cups
  11. Enjoy!
